## 2026-02-10 - [Form Accessibility & Feedback]
**Learning:** Linking labels to inputs via `htmlFor` and `id` is a fundamental a11y requirement that also significantly improves the reliability of automated end-to-end tests. Simple status-based feedback (idle -> submitting -> success) drastically improves the perceived responsiveness of the interface.
**Action:** Always ensure form elements are properly associated and provide clear visual feedback for asynchronous actions.
